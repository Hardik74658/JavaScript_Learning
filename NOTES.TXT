OPERATORS

=>operators : opearators are special symbols which are used to perform operations on operands

=>type of opearators:
- arithmatic opearators : +,-,*,/,%,++,--
- relational opearators : <,>,<=,>=,==,!=,===,!==,instanceof
- logical opearators : &&,||,!
- bitwise opearators : &,|,^,~,<<,>>,>>>
- asisgnment opearators : =,+=,-=,*=,/=,%=,<<=,>>=,&=,^=,|=
- ternary opearators : ?:
